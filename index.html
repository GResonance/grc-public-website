The Gathering Resonance Collective(GRC) is made up of community members who provide space and need-based services for learning, connecting and creating. We have a new home, upstairs, in The Pink House (2474 N. Booth). Our center is available to rent* for classes, workshops, meetings, ceremonies, gatherings and performances. We have open work hours as time permits. To be notified of open hours connect with us via Telegram and connect to GRC_Jess.

*rental of space is covered by a suggested donation. GRC can host your next event, provide childcare and/or facilitate discussions for an additional donation (for more info or to schedule, contact us at gatheringresonance@gmail.com)
